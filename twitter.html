CVS Reviews FAQs

\n

Is CVS a good company to work for?

\n

CVS has an overall rating of 3.6 Average Rating out of 5, based on over 209 CVS Review Ratings left anonymously by CVS employees, which is 8% lower than the average rating for all companies on CareerBliss. 84% of employees would recommend working at CVS.

\n

Does CVS pay their employees well?

\n

CVS employees earn $30,000\nannually on average, or $14\nper hour, which is 55% lower than the national salary average of $66,000 per year.\n99 CVS employees have shared their salaries on CareerBliss. Find CVS Salaries by Job Title.

\n

How satisfied are employees working at CVS?

\n

84% of employees would recommend working at CVS with the overall rating of 3.6 out of 5. Employees also rated CVS\n 3.4 out of 5 for Company Culture,\n 3.2 for Rewards You Receive,\n 3.1 for Growth Opportunities and\n 3.6 for support you get.

\n

What is the highest paying job at CVS?

\n

According to our data, the highest paying job at CVS is a\nVP of Marketing Strategy at $212,000 annually. Browse CVS Salaries by Job Profile.

\n

What is the lowest paying job at CVS?

\n

According to our data, the lowest paying job at CVS is a\nStock Cashier at $12,000 annually. Browse CVS Salaries by Job Profile.

\n

What are the pros and cons of working at CVS?

\n

According to reviews on CareerBliss, employees commonly rated the pros of working at CVS to be Company Culture, Growth Opportunities, People You Work With and Person You Work For, and no cons.
